WELCOME TO EXAMTREE

ExamTree is in service since 2007 and is a platform for students to submit their examination's application online.

The exams that are going to be conducted in a given month will be notified on the website on the 1st of that month.

For taking exams, the students are required to register on this website by going to the registration page. After registering, they can select the subject(s) for which they want to appear in the examination and pay the required examination fee.
